# 50 Common Health Conditions (English - Farsi)

This document contains 50 common health conditions with their English translation, Farsi translation, pronunciation, and an example sentence.

### 1. Cold
* **Farsi:** سرماخوردگی
* **Pronunciation:** Sarmakhordegi
* **Sentence (Farsi):** من سرماخورده‌ام و نیاز به استراحت دارم.
* **Sentence (English):** I have caught a cold and need to rest.

### 2. Flu
* **Farsi:** آنفولانزا
* **Pronunciation:** Anfolanza
* **Sentence (Farsi):** او به خاطر آنفولانزا به مدرسه نرفت.
* **Sentence (English):** He didn't go to school because of the flu.

### 3. Fever

* **Pronunciation:** Tab
